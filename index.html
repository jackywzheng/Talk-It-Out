"Talk it out"

is a web app that connects users, no, people, who are struggling with mental health to certified professionals who can help. Despite recent efforts at increasing mental health awareness, people are still hesitant to seek help because of social stigmatization. It can sometimes be difficult to even get out of bed, nevertheless going out of your way to see someone on the other side of town.

Our goal

is to encourage people who struggle with mental health in taking the first step and helping make treatment more accessible. Let's talk it out!

Suicide is the 3rd leading cause of death in youth ages 10 - 24. 70% of youth in state and local juvenile justice systems have a mental illness. Serious mental illness costs America $193.2 billion in lost earnings per year.
